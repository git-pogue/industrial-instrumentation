Pressure

Principles & Devices

Pressure is force per unit area (P = F/A). We measure gauge, absolute, or differential pressure. Sensing elements include Bourdon tubes, diaphragms, piezo-resistive bridges, capacitive cells, and resonant silicon.

DevicePrincipleTypical RangeNotes
Bourdon GaugeElastic tube deflection-1 to 10,000 psiRugged, local indication
DP TransmitterΔP across orifice/elementInches H2O to hundreds psiUsed for flow, level
Absolute TransmitterDiaphragm vs vacuumTorr to barVacuum/boiling studies
Seal SystemDiaphragm + capillaryProcess dependentIsolation from clogging/corrosion
